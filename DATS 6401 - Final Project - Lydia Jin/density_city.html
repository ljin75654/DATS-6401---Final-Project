Density of Restaurants by City

Similar to the country density visualizations, I just took the two countries with the greatest number of restaurants (India and United States). We can see that the results are mostly as expected. Since there are so many restaurants in India, there have to be many clustered together closely in the same cities. It's pretty unexpected for the US data, though, in that we see that there are only 5 clear cities appearing. The rest have maybe just or 2 restaurants from this data set. It seems that the distribution of restaurants in the US is nuch larger. It seems much easier for interested restaurant owners to set up shop in the US than India.
